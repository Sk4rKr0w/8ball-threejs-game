Stick Controls:
  • Right Arrow (→): Move Stick to the right direction
  • Left Arrow (←): Move Stick to the left direction
----------- Camera Controls:
  • Left Mouse Drag: Move the camera around the table
  • Right Mouse Drag: Move the camera around the environment
  • Note: Feel free to use the panel on the right section of the screen in order to play with different camera angles
----------- How to play:
  • Press Enter: Shot
  • Click on "Test a Shot!" button: Try a shot with a randomized strenght and direction (could be anywhere!)
-----------
